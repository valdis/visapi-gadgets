Bowling Gadget for Google Spreadsheets

The bowling gadget shows bowling scores on top of a spredsheet. The gadget keeps the score, and indicates who's turn it is now.

An Example


You can open the example and create your own copy.

How to use in your spreadsheet

Each player will have 2 columns, one for the first throw and one for the second.
The top row is for player names.
The next 12 rows are for actual hits.

Data Policy

All the data remains on the client, no data is sent to a server other than the Google Spreadsheets server.
